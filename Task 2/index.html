Ikigai book

'Ikigai' by Hector Garcia and Francesc Miralles explores the Japanese concept of finding one's purpose in life by analyzing the habits and beliefs of the world's longest-living people.

Through case studies, the book offers practical insights on how to live a more fulfilling life.
